Welcome to my Hobby Page!

Volleyball is a game played by two teams of six players each, in which an inflated ball is volleyed over a high net. Each team tries to make the ball touch the court within the opposing side's playing areas before it can be returned. A team is allowed to touch the ball three times before returning it. The catch is that you can only make contact with the ball for a very brief amount of time which makes an interesting premise.

Mikasa is one of the most famous volleyball brands. It's even used in the professional settings like in the FIVB (Fédération Internationale de Volleyball). Personally, this is my preferred brand as the ball is incredibly aerodynamic and has a lot of grip allowing you to generate massive spin.

What You Need To Play

There aren't a lot of things you need to play casually. The bare minimum is a pair of sneakers, a volleyball, and a net. However, if you wish to have a better, I recommend basketball shoes for more grip on the court. This will prevent you from slipping and provide comfort during intense matches. Another one is a high quality volleyball. A good brand is the Mikasa. A high quality ball goes for around 70 dollars on Amazon which can be a big investment, but, you will definitely get a better experience from using a professional ball. While I RECOMMEND the brand Mikasa, any official ball brand will do.

While the Mikasa brand is what I recommend when you are playing in-doors, sand volleyball is a different story. One good brand is the Wilson brand. You may wonder what the difference between in-door and out-door balls is, the difference is the material. Out-door balls are more durable and has more grip than the in-door ball allowing different playstyles than in-door volleyball. When you play beach, you have less mobility so the ball caters a more creative and cunning playstyle.

Why You Should Play

Volleyball, on a surface level, is often considered an easy sport. Anyone who is somewhat athletic can jump high and hit the ball hard. However, there is so much more to volleyball than just that. Outside of spiking the ball, you can set and pass. These are the hardest mechanics of volleyball. Being able to slowly learn the skills to be able to control where the ball goes with just your arms is a very exciting feeling and you can clearly feel yourself becoming better. Not to mention, similar to soccer, there are multiple ways you can hit the ball allowing the ball to move a certain way. whether it be top spin, side spin, or no spin, these are just a few things that you can learn and they're most definitely not easy.

Another reason is it can be a very good way to cool off after a work out or burn a few calories. Constantly jumping can improve various parts of your body including your arms, back, and legs.
