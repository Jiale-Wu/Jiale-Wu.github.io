Evaluation

Good Website Design: Unseen.co

A creative production agency based in the United Kingdom. They specialize in transforming brands by blending meaningful branding, compelling digital experiences, and motion productions.

  • Contrast: All of the colors blend in perfectly along with the text which seems to be default black or a very dark color. They also have one or two white texts in the corner which brings you attention to their brand name.
  • Repetition: Little to no repetition when you are looking at the interface. While there is repetition in the background, it is related to the image in the back.
  • Alignment: The clickable links are positioned in the corner of the website and on other pages of the website, the selections are visually pleasing and horizontally aligned.
  • Proximity: The clickable links are in the corners but the texts are colored to where it is noticable and easy to spot.
