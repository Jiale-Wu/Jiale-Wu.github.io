What We Do:

We design and build butter statues of all sizes for clients all over the world. As an organization, we constantly strive for perfection. We wish to bring the best butter statues to our clients. Along with all of the flavors we provide, we also can redesign or, in some cases, start the butter statue from scratch if the statue does not meet the clients expectations. Although the butter statues can be pricey, we try our best to bring it down to a price that fits the clients budget proportional to the scale of the statue.
