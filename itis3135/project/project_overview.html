Dynamic Functionality on the Website

The following dynamic functionalities will be implemented using JavaScript/jQuery to enhance user interaction

  • 1. Price Calculation:
    • Description:Allows users to select their dishes and modifications and see the prices of the said dishes
    • Webpage(s)It will be features in the menu.html
    • Reason: To allow a more convenient time for customers to see what prices are on what dishes as well as hypothetical prices with modifications to their.
    • Example URL Inspiration: Websites like Amazon where they show the prices of multiple items.
  • 2. Contact Form:
    • Description: Form that customers can fill out for various reasons.
    • Webpage(s): bottom of index.html and on the contact.html.
    • Reason: To provide customers a way to provide feedback on how the facility can improve.
    • Example URL Inspiration: Basic customer service that is shown in multiple websites.
